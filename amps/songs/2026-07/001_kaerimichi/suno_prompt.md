# Suno Prompt：帰り道（仮）

## Style Prompt（英語・長文）

Genre: Japanese pop ballad (J-pop), intimate and nostalgic, in the vein of warm piano-driven singer-songwriter pop — not EDM, not idol pop.

Mood / Emotional arc: Bittersweet and tender. Begins soft and reflective, like walking home alone at dusk replaying a happy afternoon; grows into a quietly aching longing at the chorus; the final chorus lifts into a hopeful, resolved feeling — a small courage to confess someday.

Tempo: 74 BPM. Key: C Major with frequent A-minor color for wistfulness; final chorus modulates up a half step (to D-flat) for lift.

Vocals: Solo female lead, youthful and close-mic'd, slightly breathy and conversational in the verses, opening up emotionally in the chorus, with a stronger, more determined tone in the last chorus. Natural Japanese diction, sincere delivery, not over-produced.

Instrumentation: Piano as the emotional core throughout, with gentle acoustic guitar arpeggios. Verses are sparse. Soft drums and warm bass enter at the chorus. Sustained strings swell in the choruses and thicken in the last chorus. Keep dynamics breathing — never over-compressed; preserve the quiet moments.

Structure & dynamics: Intro (piano) → Verse 1 (sparse) → Pre-Chorus (build) → Chorus (full, strings enter) → Verse 2 → Pre-Chorus → Chorus → Bridge (stripped back, intimate) → Last Chorus (key change up, strings thick, peak emotion) → Outro (subtract everything, leave a single line of piano and voice for lingering resonance).

Production: Clean, warm, spacious mix. Prioritize emotional clarity and lingering afterglow over loudness. Original composition only — do not imitate any specific existing artist or song.
